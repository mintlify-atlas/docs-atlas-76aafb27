TeamTalker uses Clerk for secure authentication, providing a seamless sign-up and sign-in experience.

## Sign Up Process

Create your TeamTalker account in just a few steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Sign Up">
    Visit the TeamTalker application and click on the sign-up option.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Your Details">
    Provide your information:
    - First name and last name
    - Email address
    - Password (secure and unique)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify Your Email">
    Check your email inbox for a verification link from Clerk and click to verify your account.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Profile Creation">
    Once verified, your profile is automatically created with:
    - Your full name (firstName + lastName)
    - Profile image (from Clerk)
    - Email address
    - Unique user ID
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Your profile is automatically generated when you first sign up. The system creates a Profile record linked to your Clerk authentication.
</Info>

## Sign In Process

Access your TeamTalker account:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Sign In Page">
    Navigate to the sign-in page at `/sign-in`.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Credentials">
    Provide your registered email and password.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Access Your Dashboard">
    After successful authentication, you'll be redirected to:
    - Your server dashboard (if you're a member of servers)
    - The initial setup modal (if you're new and haven't joined any servers)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sign Out

Safely end your session:

1. Click on your **User Avatar** in the navigation sidebar (bottom left)
2. Select **Sign Out** from the dropdown menu
3. You'll be redirected to the home page (`/`)

<Note>
  The UserButton component from Clerk provides a secure sign-out process that clears your session and authentication tokens.
</Note>

## Session Management

### Protected Routes

All routes in TeamTalker are protected by default using Clerk's authentication middleware:

<Info>
  The middleware automatically protects all routes except `/api/uploadthing`. If you're not authenticated, you'll be redirected to sign in.
</Info>

**Public Routes:**
- `/api/uploadthing` - File upload endpoint

**Protected Routes:**
- All other application routes
- All API endpoints (except uploadthing)
- Server pages and channels

### How Sessions Work

<Steps>
  <Step title="Initial Authentication">
    When you sign in, Clerk creates a secure session and stores authentication tokens.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Profile Retrieval">
    The `currentProfile()` function checks your Clerk session and retrieves your TeamTalker profile from the database.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Session Validation">
    Every protected request validates your session using the `auth()` helper from Clerk.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Automatic Redirect">
    If your session expires or is invalid, you're automatically redirected to sign in.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Profile Loading

When you first access TeamTalker:

1. The `initialProfile()` function checks if you're authenticated
2. If not authenticated, you're redirected to sign in
3. If authenticated, it looks for your existing profile in the database
4. If no profile exists, it creates one with your Clerk user data
5. Your profile is then used throughout the application

<Warning>
  Never share your authentication credentials. Clerk uses industry-standard security practices, but your password security is your responsibility.
</Warning>

## User Avatar Display

Your profile image from Clerk is displayed throughout TeamTalker:

- **Navigation Sidebar**: Bottom left corner with the UserButton
- **Member Lists**: In server member views
- **Chat Messages**: Next to your messages
- **Profile Views**: In user profiles

The avatar automatically updates when you change your profile image in Clerk.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can't Sign In">
    - Verify your email address is correct
    - Check if you've verified your email
    - Try resetting your password
    - Clear browser cache and cookies
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Session Expired">
    - Sessions expire after a period of inactivity
    - Simply sign in again to create a new session
    - Your data and servers are preserved
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Profile Not Loading">
    - Ensure you're signed in to Clerk
    - Check your internet connection
    - Try refreshing the page
    - Sign out and sign back in
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
