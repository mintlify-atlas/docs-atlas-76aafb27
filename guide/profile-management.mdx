Your TeamTalker profile represents your identity across all servers and channels. Learn how to view and manage your profile information.

## Your Profile Information

Your TeamTalker profile stores the following information:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="User ID" icon="fingerprint">
    Unique identifier linked to your Clerk account
  </Card>
  <Card title="Name" icon="signature">
    Your display name (first + last name)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Email" icon="envelope">
    Your verified email address
  </Card>
  <Card title="Profile Image" icon="image">
    Your avatar displayed across TeamTalker
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Profile Data Structure

Your profile includes:

- **Unique Profile ID**: UUID generated when your profile is created
- **User ID**: Links to your Clerk authentication account
- **Display Name**: Combined first and last name from Clerk
- **Profile Image URL**: Avatar image from Clerk
- **Email Address**: Verified email from your Clerk account
- **Created At**: Timestamp when you joined TeamTalker
- **Updated At**: Last time your profile was modified

<Info>
  Your profile is automatically created when you first sign up and is synced with your Clerk account information.
</Info>

## Viewing Your Profile

You can view your profile information in several places:

### Navigation Sidebar

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate Your Avatar">
    Find your profile avatar at the bottom of the left navigation sidebar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Your Avatar">
    Click on the UserButton to open your profile menu.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Profile Options">
    Access profile settings, account management, and sign-out options.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Server Member Lists

Your profile appears in server member lists showing:
- Your profile image
- Your display name
- Your email address
- Your role badge (Admin, Moderator, or Guest)

<Note>
  Your profile information is consistent across all servers you're a member of.
</Note>

## Editing Your Profile

Profile updates are managed through your Clerk account, which automatically syncs with TeamTalker.

### Update Your Name

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Clerk Settings">
    Click your avatar in the navigation sidebar and select account settings.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Edit Name Fields">
    Update your first name and/or last name in the Clerk profile settings.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Your display name in TeamTalker updates automatically based on the combined first and last name.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Update Your Profile Image

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Clerk Profile">
    Navigate to your Clerk account settings via the UserButton.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Upload New Image">
    Click on your current avatar and upload a new profile picture.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Automatic Sync">
    The new image syncs automatically to TeamTalker and appears across all servers.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Profile image changes may take a few moments to propagate across all views in the application.
</Warning>

### Update Your Email

Email updates must be done through Clerk:

1. Access your Clerk account settings
2. Add or change your email address
3. Verify the new email via the verification link
4. The updated email automatically syncs to your TeamTalker profile

<Info>
  Email verification is required for security. You'll receive a verification link in your new email inbox.
</Info>

## Profile Avatar Display

Your avatar image is displayed in multiple locations throughout TeamTalker:

### Display Locations

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Navigation Sidebar">
    Your avatar appears in the UserButton at the bottom left of the navigation sidebar with:
    - Size: 48x48 pixels
    - Rounded circle style
    - Click to access account settings
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Member Lists">
    When viewing server members:
    - Size: 7-10 responsive sizing (smaller on mobile)
    - Displays next to your name
    - Shows your role badge
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Server Sidebar">
    In server member views:
    - Appears next to your username
    - Shows online/offline status (when implemented)
    - Indicates your role in the server
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Avatar Component

TeamTalker uses a custom `UserAvatar` component that:
- Displays your profile image from the `imageUrl` field
- Provides responsive sizing for different screen sizes
- Uses fallback behavior if image fails to load
- Maintains consistent styling across the application

## Profile Privacy

Understand what information is visible to other users:

### Visible to All Server Members

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Display Name" icon="user">
    Your full name is visible to all members in shared servers
  </Card>
  <Card title="Profile Image" icon="image">
    Your avatar is shown in member lists and chat
  </Card>
  <Card title="Email Address" icon="envelope">
    Visible in member management views (Admin access)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Server Role" icon="shield">
    Your role badge is displayed next to your name
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  Your email address is visible to server administrators in the member management interface.
</Note>

### Private Information

- **User ID**: Internal identifier, not displayed to users
- **Profile ID**: Database identifier, not shown in UI
- **Authentication Details**: Managed securely by Clerk

## Retrieving Your Profile Data

For developers or API access, you can retrieve your profile:

### API Endpoint

```bash
GET /api/profile
```

**Response:**
```json
{
  "id": "profile-uuid",
  "userId": "clerk-user-id",
  "name": "John Doe",
  "imageUrl": "https://clerk.com/avatar.jpg",
  "email": "john@example.com",
  "createdAt": "2024-01-01T00:00:00.000Z",
  "updatedAt": "2024-01-15T00:00:00.000Z"
}
```

<Info>
  You must be authenticated to access this endpoint. Unauthorized requests return a 401 status.
</Info>

## Profile Relationships

Your profile is connected to other entities in TeamTalker:

### Server Ownership

- **Servers Created**: List of servers where you're the owner
- **Server Count**: Track how many servers you've created
- **Management Rights**: Full control over your created servers

### Server Memberships

- **Member Records**: One membership per server you've joined
- **Role Assignment**: Each membership has an associated role
- **Server Access**: Memberships determine which servers you can access

<Note>
  A single profile can have multiple server memberships, each with different roles.
</Note>

## Profile Creation Process

Understand how your profile is created:

<Steps>
  <Step title="First Sign In">
    When you first sign in after creating a Clerk account, TeamTalker checks for an existing profile.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Profile Lookup">
    The system searches for a profile linked to your Clerk user ID.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Auto-Creation">
    If no profile exists, TeamTalker automatically creates one with:
    - Your Clerk user ID
    - Combined first and last name
    - Profile image URL from Clerk
    - Email address from Clerk
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Database Storage">
    Your profile is saved in the PostgreSQL database with timestamps.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Profile Image Not Updating">
    - Clear browser cache and refresh
    - Verify the image was updated in Clerk
    - Wait a few minutes for sync to complete
    - Try signing out and back in
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Name Not Displaying Correctly">
    - Check your first and last name in Clerk settings
    - Ensure both fields are populated
    - Update in Clerk and refresh TeamTalker
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Profile Data Missing">
    - Verify you're signed in to Clerk
    - Check if your profile was created (sign out and back in)
    - Contact support if profile creation fails
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
